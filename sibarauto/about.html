Corporate Profile

Over Four Decades of Metallurgy Legacy

Established in 1983, SIBAR Auto Parts Limited is a leading manufacturer of precision aluminium castings and engineered automotive components.

Our Legacy

Leading Since 1983

SIBAR Auto Parts Limited stands at the forefront of the precision casting industry in India. Founded with a vision to deliver world-class gravity and low-pressure castings, we have spent decades perfecting our metallurgical engineering to serve as a critical supplier to global automotive OEMs and industrial corporations.

From our advanced facility in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh, we merge state-of-the-art robotic automation with deep metallurgical expertise. Our processes conform to rigorous international quality parameters, ensuring absolute reliability in every block, head, and housing we engineer.

Factory Infrastructure

World-Class Manufacturing Facility

Strategically located in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh, our manufacturing plant features separate departments for Gravity Die Casting (GDC), Low Pressure Die Casting (LPDC), CNC Machining centers, and Electro Plating workshops.

Our layout utilizes vertical integration from casting to hard chrome plating and nickel-silicon plating, providing a one-stop-shop capability for cylinder heads, engine blocks, and industrial housings.
