About Sibar

Over Four Decades of Casting Legacy

SIBAR Auto Parts Limited is one of India's leading manufacturers of high-precision aluminium castings and machined automotive components. Strategically headquartered in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh, we supply critical components to top-tier automotive and industrial clients worldwide, holding an uncompromising commitment to quality and engineering reliability.

Capabilities

World-Class Manufacturing

From complex gravity casting to ultra-precise surface engineering, we have end-to-end facilities under one roof.

Gravity Die Casting

Ideal for manufacturing medium to high volume precision components with excellent mechanical qualities and dimensional control.

Low Pressure Die Casting

Ensures high-density, high-strength casting structures suitable for complex shapes, crucial for automotive applications.

Precision Machining

Equipped with advanced multi-axis CNC machines and machining centers to deliver highly precise component specifications.

Hard Chrome Plating

Delivers exceptional hardness, wear resistance, and low friction qualities to critical engineering components.

Nickel Silicon Coating

High-tech surface engineering providing superior durability and performance upgrades for high-friction cylinders.

Quality Inspection

Equipped with strict QA systems, 3D coordinate measuring machines (CMM), spectrographs, and testing labs to ensure zero-defect delivery.
