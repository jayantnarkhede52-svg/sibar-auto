Frequently Asked Questions

Key details regarding lead times, casting weights, and listing audits.

We specialized in Gravity Die Casting (GDC) for high-integrity dense blocks, Low Pressure Die Casting (LPDC) for control fillings, and Centrifugal Casting for cylinders. CNC precision machining and surface chrome electro-plating are fully integrated on-site.

Our die-casting equipment can cast aluminium parts weighing up to 25 kilograms with custom envelope dimensions up to 600mm x 600mm x 400mm. Sizing tolerances are inspected down to ±5 microns on VMC lines.

Every melt batch undergoes spectrometer analysis prior to casting, and all castings are stamped with unique heat numbers. Dimensional reports, density values, and pressure test certifications are archived to secure traceability.
